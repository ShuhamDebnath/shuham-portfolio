About Us

Welcome to
Shuham Debnath.
Indie development from India.

We create clean, reliable, and user-friendly mobile apps for Android and iOS.

Every app we build is a product of curiosity, problem-solving, and a commitment to privacy and performance.

person Know More About Us

We are a passionate team dedicated to building tools that make everyday life simpler and more organised. We take a privacy-first approach — every app we ship stores its data locally on your device unless there's a compelling reason to do otherwise.

When we're not coding, we're exploring new app ideas, learning about design systems, and figuring out how to make complex things feel effortless. CareLog is our first published app, and it's just the start.
